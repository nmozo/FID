About Pixel Harbor Studios

Who we are

Pixel Harbor Studios is a small, passionate team of designers, programmers, and artists who believe games can be both nostalgic and innovative. Our workflow blends retro aesthetics with cutting-edge tools.

How we began

Founded in 2016 by a group of university game design classmates, our first release began as a small game jam entry. Since then, we’ve expanded into full-production indie development.
